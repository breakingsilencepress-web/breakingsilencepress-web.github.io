01

Who We Are

BreakingSilence Press is an independent investigative publishing project based in Islamabad, Pakistan. We publish research, evidence, and analysis related to the U.S. Invention Secrecy Act and related government policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information of visitors who subscribe to updates on our website.

02

What We Collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary:

  • Your email address, submitted voluntarily through our subscription form
  • The date and time you subscribed

We do not collect names, payment information, IP addresses, browsing behavior, or any other personal data. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

03

How We Use Your Email

Your email address is used exclusively to send you:

  • A one-time verification email to confirm your subscription
  • Updates about new chapter releases and evidence publications
  • Occasional research announcements related to BreakingSilence Press

We will never send you promotional content, advertisements, or third-party offers. Every email we send is directly related to the research and content of this project.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your email address with any third party under any circumstances. Your data is stored securely in our private database and is accessible only to the site administrator.

05

Data Retention

Your email address is retained for as long as you remain subscribed. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly. Upon request, we will permanently remove your email from our database within 7 days.

06

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent to receive emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

07

Security

Subscriber data is stored in a secured, access-controlled database. Only the site administrator has access. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

08

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the subscription service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
