Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

1. PURPOSE & TONE

Purpose
This Privacy Policy explains how Breaking Silence collects, uses, and protects visitor information when users access chapter content, scan QR codes from the book, or view document evidence hosted on the site.

Tone

  • Clear, restrained, and non‑exploitative
  • Trust‑forward and reader‑centric
  • Consistent with investigative nonfiction and public‑interest scholarship
  • Explicitly avoids marketing language or data‑monetization framing

2. SCOPE OF COVERAGE

This policy applies to:

  • The main Breaking Silence website
  • Chapter pages (including Chapter 1)
  • Document evidence landing pages
  • QR‑code‑linked resources from the book
  • Embedded PDFs and document previews

It does not apply to external sites linked from the website.

3. INFORMATION COLLECTED

A. Information Collected Automatically

The website may automatically collect limited, non‑identifying technical data, including:

  • IP address (anonymized or truncated where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL (e.g., QR scan, direct visit)
  • Pages viewed and time spent
  • Approximate geographic region (country or city level only)

Purpose

This data is used solely to:

  • Understand which chapters and documents are being accessed
  • Verify QR‑code functionality
  • Detect broken links or unavailable documents
  • Improve site stability and accessibility

No automated profiling or behavioral tracking is performed.

B. Information You Voluntarily Provide

The website may collect information you explicitly choose to provide, such as:

  • Email address (only if a contact or update form is used)
  • Message content (if you submit feedback or corrections)

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry or request.

4. WHAT IS NOT COLLECTED (IMPORTANT)

Breaking Silence does not:

  • Sell or rent user data
  • Collect names, phone numbers, or addresses by default
  • Use advertising trackers
  • Use social‑media pixels
  • Build marketing profiles
  • Track users across unrelated websites
  • Use invasive fingerprinting techniques

No data is collected for advertising or commercial surveillance.

5. COOKIES & ANALYTICS

Cookies
The site may use:

  • Essential cookies for basic functionality
  • Session cookies to maintain page navigation

No third‑party advertising cookies are used.

Analytics
If analytics tools are used, they are configured to:

  • Minimize data retention
  • Avoid cross‑site tracking
  • Aggregate usage data only

Visitors are never identified as individuals.

6. DOCUMENTS, PDFs & QR CODES

Document Access
When you:

  • Scan a QR code from the book
  • Visit a document evidence page
  • View or download a PDF

The site may log:

  • That the document was accessed
  • The date and general location (non‑precise)

Reason

This ensures:

  • Long‑term availability of cited sources
  • Protection against link rot
  • Preservation of academic and journalistic integrity

Documents are provided for educational and public‑interest purposes.

7. THIRD‑PARTY LINKS

The website may link to:

  • Government archives
  • Academic institutions
  • Court records
  • Journalistic sources

Once you leave the site, their privacy policies apply.
Breaking Silence is not responsible for external data practices.

8. DATA SECURITY

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to:

  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Prevent data misuse or alteration
  • Maintain site integrity

However, no internet system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

9. DATA RETENTION

  • Technical logs are retained only as long as necessary for site maintenance and research integrity.
  • Contact communications are retained only to complete correspondence, unless ongoing dialogue is requested.

10. USER RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data you provided
  • Request correction or deletion of such data
  • Ask how your data is being used

Requests can be made via the site's contact page.

11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This website is intended for a general adult audience.
No knowing collection of data from children under 13 occurs.

12. POLICY CHANGES

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect:

  • Legal requirements
  • Technical changes
  • Expansion of site features

The "Last Updated" date will always be shown at the bottom.

13. CONTACT INFORMATION

For privacy‑related questions or concerns:

Contact via the website's official contact page.
