Context

This patent discloses a defensive aircraft countermeasure that neutralizes infrared heat-seeking missiles by dispersing a high‑pressure fluid spray into the exhaust plume, degrading the missile’s infrared guidance system. It demonstrates that non‑explosive, non‑kinetic technologies capable of significantly altering military and civilian aircraft survivability were technically feasible and formally documented. In Chapter 1, this patent supports the claim that transformative defensive innovations—particularly those intersecting with national security—exist within the patent system and are subject to heightened scrutiny, secrecy regimes, or constrained deployment despite their civilian safety implications.
