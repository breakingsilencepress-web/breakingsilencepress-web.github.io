The Document:

Patent US 8,925,438 B1
"Countermeasure Technique Using an Appropriate Fluid Spray Against Infrared Heat Seeking Missile"
Inventors: Budimir & Desanka Damnjanovic
Filed: 2007 - Issued: 2015 (after secrecy lifted)

Blocked from Publication

The patent was completely withheld from public view for national security reasons.

No Foreign Filing

Inventors were prohibited from filing the patent in any other country.

Complete Secrecy

Even the existence of the patent was classified information.

About The Book

Delve into the fascinating world of classified military technology with this unprecedented look at a real U.S. patent that was kept secret for national security reasons. This book explores the invention, the inventors, the technology behind infrared countermeasures, and the implications of government secrecy on innovation.

Through meticulous research and exclusive interviews, the author uncovers the story behind Patent US 8,925,438 B1 and what it reveals about the intersection of technology, national security, and public knowledge.

Discover how two inventors created a revolutionary defense technology, only to have their work classified for years. Learn about the technical challenges they overcame and the implications of their invention for modern warfare.

Timeline of Secrecy
